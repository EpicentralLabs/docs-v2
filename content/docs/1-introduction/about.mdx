## The Mission

Our mission to build the necessary infrastructure to make options trading possible on the [Solana Blockchain](https://solana.com/). 
There's no better way to bring new decentralized finance derivatives to Solana than by doing so through our [Epicentral DAO](/docs/2-epicentral-dao).

We plan to expand option market capabilities across the ecosystem by not only building flagship products (OPX) but also creating open-source SDKs for other protocols to integrate options trading into their own products.

## The Problem

Current market opportunities allow traders to profit by either trading at spot price or with leverage via [perpetual futures](https://www.investopedia.com/what-are-perpetual-futures-7494870) (perp trading). 
While this is a great start for traders who are ambitious and enjoy greater risk for even greater rewards, are still missing many key factors that could benefit them more with more calculated risk. 

**As a result, factors like:**
- accruing borrow interest on leveraged positions,
- undefined volatility,
- and minimal risk management (only offering stop-losses, take-profits, etc.),
are all hindering the potential for traders to make smarter trades with predefined risk and reward.

## The Solution

By embracing our [Core Philosophy](#the-core-philosophy), options trading addresses much of today's excessive risk exposure by providing a more structured and predictable trading experience.

**Options are tools that:**
- mitigate over-exposure to market volatility,
- provide a measurable trading experience with predefined risk and reward,
- and offer a more advanced risk management strategies for traders who want to take their trading to the next level.
