# Origin

Epicentral Labs, DAO LLC is a limited liability company and decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) organized as per the laws of the State of Wyoming pursuant to the **Wyoming Limited Liability Company Act**, [*W.S. 17-29-101 et seq.*](https://sos.wyo.gov/Forms/WyoBiz/Wyoming_Limited_Liability_Company_Act_and_Close_LLC_Supplement.pdf) Founded by [TheLazySol](https://x.com/TheLazySol) on **July 6th, 2024**.

The name “Epicentral”, comes from the word *"epicenter"*, symbolizing to be the focal point for innovation in decentralized finance and blockchain development.
