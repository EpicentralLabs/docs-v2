## Overview

Epicentral DAO ("Epicentral Labs") is the on-chain infrastructure in how Epicentral Labs **operates as a whole**. Most includes, but not limited to: 

- development financing, 
- partnerships, 
- collaborations, 
- payment to contributors, and 
- treasury asset allocations.

Specifically, Epicentral DAO is built on the [Solana blockchain](https://solana.com/) and can be accessed and governed through [Realms](https://realms.today/), the leading open-source platform for DAO creation, governance, and treasury management on Solana.

|----------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|

| [Create a Proposal](https://docs.realms.today/realms-v2/features/proposals)                           | 250,000 LABS          |
| Draft a Proposal ([Discord](https://discord.gg/5asAuY2sR8) Forum Post)        | 0 LABS                |
| Edit DAO Configuration                      | 1,500,000 LABS        |

### Who can participate?

**LABS Token Holders** can vote, comment, discuss, and create proposals in the DAO. 
Anyone who does not meet the minimum token requirement to create a proposal directly on-chain (to prevent spam), can still create a draft proposal in the [Discord](https://discord.gg/5asAuY2sR8) server.
