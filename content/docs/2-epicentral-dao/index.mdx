# The DAO

Epicentral DAO ("Epicentral Labs") is the on-chain infrastructure in how Epicentral Labs **operates as a whole**. Most includes, but not limited to: 

- development financing, 
- partnerships, 
- collaborations, 
- payment to contributors, and 
- treasury asset allocations.

Specifically, Epicentral DAO is on the Solana Blockchain
