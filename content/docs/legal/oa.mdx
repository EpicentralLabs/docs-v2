## Overview

The Operating Agreement is a document that outlines the rules and regulations for the operation of Epicentral Labs, DAO LLC and its products. It is a legal document that is used to govern and enforce the operations of the company and its products -- ensuring that the Members, [Core Team Members](/docs/epicentral-dao/core-team), and [LABS](/docs/tokens/labs-token) Token Holders, operate in a lawful and compliant manner.

The full operating agreement can be found in the [legal repository](https://github.com/EpicentralLabs/legal) in our Epicentral Labs GitHub organization.
